What is Academies General Annual Grant 2022 to 2023
The Education and Skills Funding Agency (ESFA) allocates funding every year to open academies, including free schools, university technical colleges (UTCs), special schools and alternative provision (AP) academies. Each academy receives a general annual grant allocation statement, which is uploaded to the ESFA’s Information Exchange portal, but is planned to soon be issued as an online statement via Manage Your Education and Skills Funding (MYESF). This specification includes the calculation of al grants paid as General Annual Grant and included on the allocation statement for the academic year. The exception being any funding paid to providers opening in the period 1 April to 31 August of the Academic year as their funding is calculated on a different basis and will be covered in a separate specification for the period to 31 August.
